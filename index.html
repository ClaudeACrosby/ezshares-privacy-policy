EzShares Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

1. Overview

EzShares is a productivity app that helps users create and share calendar events using their Google account. We respect your privacy and do not store or share your personal data outside of Google’s secure APIs.

2. Information We Access

  • Google account email address (for authentication)
  • Calendar event data that you explicitly create or share

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used only to create and manage events on your Google Calendar at your request. EzShares does not store, transmit, or sell your information to any third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

All sign-in and calendar interactions occur through Google’s OAuth 2.0 system. No event data or tokens are stored on EzShares servers. Authentication is handled directly between your device and Google.

5. Revoking Access

You may revoke EzShares’ access to your Google account at any time through your Google Account settings under “Third-party apps with account access.”

6. Contact

If you have any questions or privacy requests, contact us at: support@ezshares.app
